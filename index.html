Hey! My name is Steven and I am a computer scientist currently doing my master's degree with a focus on Data and Knowledge Management at Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin. I am especially interested in:
  • Event-driven architectures
  • (Distributed) Complex event processing
  • Streaming algorithms (e.g., probabilistic data structures)
In addition, I am a theoretical computer science enthusiast, whereby I am particularly interested in topics concerning:
  • Automata theory and Formal languages
  • {Parameterized, Fine-grained, Descriptive} Complexity theory
  • NP-hard optimization problems
  • Problems in NP ∩ coNP without polynomial-time solutions (I share Jack Edmonds opinion: NP ≠ NP ∩ coNP = P)
  • Graph theory
